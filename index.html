Hello there! I'm Elihu Conant-Haque!

I am a recent graduate from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, primarily interested in aeronautical engineering and model aviation. With several years of experience as a hobbyist building model aircraft, I am well-versed in practical skills, principles of aircraft design, and CAD/CAM.

I am an avid language learner, with fluent, literate proficiency in English, German and Mandarin Chinese, as well as functional knowledge of Cantonese, French, Swedish, and Spanish.

I am also a proficient tuba player, having won prestigious solo competitions such as the Lois Lymann Concerto Competition and played in ensembles such as the NY AllState Symphony and Empire State Youth Orchestra.
